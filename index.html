About Me

I am Rakib Ahmed, a dedicated Brand & Logo Designer with a focus on building minimalist and high-impact brand identities. Over the past 2 years, I have helped diverse businesses translate their vision into timeless visual symbols.

My design philosophy is rooted in minimalism and structural precision. I believe that a great logo is not just a mark—it is a strategic asset that builds trust and defines the soul of a business.

Throughout my journey, I have worked with clients across various industries, ensuring that every design I deliver is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and scalable.

Design Process

01. Research

Deep diving into your business, target audience, and market competition to find the core message.

02. Sketch

Translating concepts into hand-drawn sketches to explore various symbolic directions.

03. Refine

Vectorizing the best concepts and fine-tuning geometry, typography, and visual balance.

04. Delivery

Delivering the complete identity suite with all necessary formats and usage guidelines.

About Me

I am Rakib Ahmed, a dedicated Brand & Logo Designer with a focus on building minimalist and high-impact brand identities. Over the past 2 years, I have helped diverse businesses translate their vision into timeless visual symbols.

My design philosophy is rooted in minimalism and structural precision. I believe that a great logo is not just a mark—it is a strategic asset that builds trust and defines the soul of a business.
